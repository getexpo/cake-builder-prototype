# MVP Scope - Cake Shop Platform

## Goal
Launch the smallest useful version that lets cake shops accept and manage custom cake orders through a visual builder.

## In scope

### Customer
- Visual cake builder
- Simple mode for fast ordering with guided presets and fewer visible choices
- Advanced mode for deep customization with broader cake types and fuller layer-by-layer controls
- Ingredient and size selection
- Live pricing preview
- Save design draft
- Add to cart
- Checkout
- Pickup slot selection
- Customer notification checkpoints

### Shop owner
- Owner login
- Shop workspace
- Cake template management
- Ingredient group management
- Pricing rules
- Incoming order dashboard
- Order status updates

### Platform
- Multi-tenant data separation
- Auth
- Basic notifications
- Payment integration placeholder

## Not in MVP
- Full delivery network
- Driver app
- Advanced drag/drop decorations
- Deep analytics
- Marketplace discovery
- Complex loyalty systems

## MVP success test
A cake shop owner can configure the builder, a customer can place a custom cake order, and the owner can manage that order from receipt to pickup.
