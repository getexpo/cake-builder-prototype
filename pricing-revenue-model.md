# Pricing and Revenue Model - Cake Shop Platform

## Revenue streams
- Monthly SaaS fee per shop
- Per-order transaction fee
- Delivery margin later
- Premium plan for advanced features

## Example assumptions
- 100 shops
- $99 per month base subscription
- 120 orders per shop per month
- $55 average order value
- 3% platform take rate

## Example monthly revenue
- SaaS: 100 x 99 = $9,900
- GMV: 100 x 120 x 55 = $660,000
- Take rate revenue: 3% of $660,000 = $19,800
- Combined monthly revenue = $29,700

## What to model next
- Churn
- CAC
- Support cost
- Payment fees
- Margin after delivery expansion

## Strategy
Start SaaS plus transaction fee. Add delivery revenue later if operationally viable.
